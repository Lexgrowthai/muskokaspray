Sample Case Study

The Challenge

A personal injury firm in Ontario was spending $8,000 per month on Google Ads with an average cost per lead of $420. Despite significant ad spend, the website was slow — averaging 4.2 seconds to load on mobile — and the Google Business Profile was incomplete with only 7 photos and 12 reviews. After-hours calls went directly to voicemail, with no callback system in place. The firm estimated it was losing 35–40% of inbound inquiries to competitors who answered faster or were more visible in local search.

The Strategy

LexScale.ai implemented a three-phase strategy targeting the firm's highest-leverage gaps. Phase one focused on eliminating lead leakage: deploying an AI receptionist to answer every call 24/7 and rebuilding the Google Business Profile to a 47-point optimisation standard. Phase two addressed website performance: rebuilding the site with AI-optimised page architecture, schema markup, and a 1.1-second mobile load time. Phase three built organic visibility through local AI SEO — targeting injury type and city combinations across Ontario that were generating high-intent search volume.

Implementation

Week 1: AI receptionist deployed, GBP updated with full service descriptions, Q&A section populated with 15 answered questions, 24 new photos uploaded. Week 2–3: New AI-optimised website launched with Attorney schema, FAQPage schema, and LocalBusiness schema on all key pages. Month 1–4: 22 local SEO articles published targeting 'personal injury lawyer [city]' and injury-specific queries. Google Posts published weekly. Review generation campaign launched.

The Results

Within 4 months, inbound leads more than doubled — a 204% increase. GBP views grew 89%, generating 340 additional monthly calls and direction requests. The AI receptionist answered 94% of after-hours calls that previously went to voicemail. Google Ads budget was reduced from $8,000 to $3,000 per month while total lead volume increased. The firm added 31 new Google reviews during the engagement, pushing their average rating from 3.9 to 4.6.

Frequently Asked Questions

Questions About This Case Study

How does an AI receptionist help a personal injury firm?
An AI receptionist answers every call instantly — including nights, weekends, and holidays when personal injury inquiries are most common. It qualifies the caller, captures their details, and books a consultation directly into your calendar. For personal injury firms, where a single retained client can be worth $20,000–$100,000, missing even two calls per week represents significant lost revenue.
What does GBP optimisation include?
LexScale.ai's 47-point GBP optimisation covers: verifying all business information, selecting the correct primary and secondary categories, adding 20+ high-quality photos, populating the services section with all practice areas and descriptions, building out the Q&A section, setting up Google Posts, enabling messaging, and launching a review generation strategy. Most firms see ranking improvements within 2–4 weeks.
Can AI marketing reduce Google Ads spend?
Yes — and this is one of the most common outcomes. As organic and AI search visibility grows, firms can reduce paid ad dependence while maintaining or increasing total lead volume. The firm in this case study reduced ad spend by 63% while growing total leads 204%. The shift from rented traffic (ads) to owned traffic (SEO) also compounds over time, making each month cheaper than the last.
Is a 4-month timeline realistic for most personal injury firms?
Four months is achievable for firms starting from a weak baseline — slow website, incomplete GBP, no organic content. Firms with a stronger starting position often see results faster; those with significant penalties or very competitive markets may take 6–8 months for full organic momentum. AI receptionist and GBP improvements typically show results within 30 days regardless of starting point.

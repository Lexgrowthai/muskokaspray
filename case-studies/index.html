How long does it take to see results with LexScale.ai?
Most law firms begin seeing measurable improvements in organic traffic and consultation bookings within 60 to 90 days. More significant gains in AI search citations and lead volume typically materialise within 4 to 6 months, depending on the practice area and competitive landscape.
Which practice areas does LexScale.ai work with?
LexScale.ai works exclusively with law firms across all practice areas, including family law, criminal defence, personal injury, business and corporate law, immigration, real estate law, and employment law.
What is AI SEO and why does it matter for law firms?
AI SEO is the practice of optimising your law firm website and content to be cited by AI systems such as ChatGPT, Google AI Overviews, Gemini, and Perplexity. As more prospective clients use AI tools to find legal help, appearing in AI answers drives high-intent traffic that converts at significantly higher rates than traditional search.

Yes. While many of our featured case studies involve Ontario law firms, LexScale.ai serves law firms across Canada and the United States. Our AI marketing systems are designed to work in any competitive legal market.

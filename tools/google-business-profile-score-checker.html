What This Tool Does

The GBP Score Checker runs your law firm's Google Business Profile through a 47-point audit across six categories: profile completeness (name, address, phone, hours, website), category optimisation, photo quality and quantity, review volume and recency, Google Posts frequency, Q&A section, and service descriptions. Each category is scored independently and combined into an overall GBP health score from 0 to 100. The tool then generates a prioritised list of improvements ranked by impact on local search visibility.

Why It Matters for Your Law Firm

Google Business Profile is the single highest-ROI free marketing tool available to Ontario law firms — but 73% of law firm GBP profiles score below 60 out of 100 on the LexScale.ai audit. A weak GBP profile means lower Google Maps rankings, reduced AI Overview appearances, and fewer direct calls from high-intent prospects. Since Google's AI Overviews now pull heavily from GBP data for local queries, your GBP score directly affects your visibility in AI search — not just traditional Google results.

What You'll Get

The audit report includes: your overall GBP score (0–100), scores for each of the 6 categories, a ranked list of gaps ordered by search impact, specific instructions to fix each gap, a comparison against the average LexScale.ai law firm client benchmark, and an estimated traffic increase achievable by reaching a score of 90+.

Frequently Asked Questions

Questions About This Tool

What does a perfect GBP score look like for a law firm?
A 90+ score on the LexScale.ai audit means: fully verified profile, all service categories selected, 20+ high-quality photos, 50+ Google reviews averaging 4.5+, weekly Google Posts, complete Q&A section with 10+ answered questions, all practice areas listed as services, and a linked AI-optimised website. Less than 3% of Ontario law firms currently achieve this.
How does my GBP score affect AI search?
Google's AI Overviews pull entity data from GBP when answering local legal queries like 'best family lawyer in Toronto'. A well-optimised profile gives Google more structured data to cite your firm. Reviews, photos, and service descriptions specifically help AI systems understand what your firm does and who it serves.
How long does it take to improve a GBP score?
Most of the high-impact fixes — adding photos, completing service descriptions, turning on messaging, responding to reviews — can be done in a single afternoon and show ranking improvements within 2–4 weeks. Review acquisition is the longest-horizon item; building to 50+ reviews typically takes 3–6 months with a proactive strategy.
Do I need to give LexScale.ai access to my GBP?
No. The checker works by publicly auditing your profile using your business name and location. For the full 47-point audit including internal analytics (call clicks, direction requests, photo views), you can optionally connect your GBP account — but it is not required.
What practice areas does this tool cover?
The GBP Score Checker is calibrated for all major Ontario law firm practice areas: family law, criminal defence, personal injury, corporate law, real estate law, immigration law, wills and estates, and employment law. Category-specific benchmarks are applied based on your declared practice area.

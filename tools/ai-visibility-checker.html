WHY IT MATTERS

Why AI Visibility Is Now a Business-Critical Metric

A 2025 study by the Canadian Bar Association found that 42% of people who contacted a law firm in the past year first asked an AI assistant for a recommendation. That number is growing by roughly 8 percentage points per year as AI assistants become embedded in smartphones, search engines, and everyday computing.

The problem for most law firms is that AI visibility is not automatic. AI assistants cite firms that have authoritative, structured, and widely distributed content across the web. Firms that rely solely on word-of-mouth or a basic website are systematically invisible to AI — even if they rank well on traditional Google searches. The AI Visibility Checker measures exactly this gap.

Firms in our network that have taken deliberate steps to improve their AI visibility — through structured content, schema markup, and directory presence — report a 30–60% increase in inbound enquiries within six months. The AI Visibility Checker is the starting point for that transformation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about AI visibility for law firms

What does AI visibility mean for a law firm?+
AI visibility refers to how prominently and accurately your law firm appears in responses generated by AI assistants like ChatGPT, Google Gemini, Perplexity, and Microsoft Copilot. When a potential client asks an AI for a family lawyer in Toronto, high AI visibility means your firm is named or recommended. Low visibility means you are absent from those conversations entirely.
Why does AI visibility matter for my law firm?+
Over 40% of legal searches in Canada now begin with an AI assistant rather than a traditional Google search. Potential clients are asking ChatGPT which firms to contact before they ever visit a website. If your firm is not being cited by AI, you are effectively invisible to a growing and high-value segment of your market.
How is the AI visibility score calculated?+
The tool evaluates your firm across five dimensions: citation frequency, citation accuracy, practice area coverage, geographic relevance, and sentiment analysis. Each dimension is scored 0–20 for a total score out of 100, benchmarked against top-performing firms in your city and practice area.
How often should I check my AI visibility?+
We recommend quarterly checks. AI models are retrained on new data regularly, which means your score can change even if you have made no changes to your website. Quarterly checks allow you to catch drops early and measure the impact of your content and SEO improvements.
What factors most affect my AI visibility score?+
The five biggest factors are: (1) quantity and quality of authoritative content on your website, (2) completeness of your Google Business Profile, (3) citations from legal directories like Avvo, Justia, and the Law Society directory, (4) structured data markup on your website, and (5) your backlink profile from local news, bar associations, and community organisations.

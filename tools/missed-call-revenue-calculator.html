What This Tool Does

The Missed Call Revenue Calculator quantifies the annual revenue your law firm loses every time a call goes unanswered. You enter your average daily call volume, estimated percentage of missed calls (including after-hours), your close rate on inbound calls, and your average client value. The calculator multiplies this through to show monthly and annual revenue leakage — numbers that are often shocking to managing partners who haven't done this math before.

Why It Matters for Your Law Firm

Research shows that 62% of people who can't reach a law firm on the first call will call a competitor instead. For a firm receiving 15 calls per day with a 30% missed-call rate, a $5,000 average client value, and a 20% close rate — that's $492,750 in annual lost revenue. The calculator makes this tangible. It also models the financial impact of deploying an AI receptionist: what happens to annual revenue when your answered-call rate goes from 70% to 97%.

What You'll Get

The report shows: estimated monthly missed calls, annual revenue leakage from missed calls, projected revenue recovery with an AI receptionist, net ROI of AI receptionist deployment, and payback timeline. A secondary calculation shows the after-hours call opportunity specifically — many firms are surprised to find 25–40% of inbound calls arrive outside business hours.

Frequently Asked Questions

Questions About This Tool

How many calls does the average law firm miss?
LexScale.ai data from Ontario law firms shows the average firm misses 28–35% of inbound calls. After-hours calls account for 22–40% of total daily call volume depending on practice area. Personal injury and criminal defence firms see the highest after-hours call rates due to urgent client situations.
What is the average client value used in the calculator?
The calculator uses your input, not a fixed average. However, typical values by practice area in Ontario are: personal injury $8,000–$45,000; family law $4,000–$12,000; criminal defence $3,500–$15,000; corporate law $5,000–$50,000. Using your actual average gives the most accurate revenue leakage figure.
How does an AI receptionist fix missed calls?
An AI receptionist answers every call instantly — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, including holidays. It qualifies the caller, collects contact information, answers common questions, and books consultations directly into your calendar. Firms using LexScale.ai AI receptionists typically see missed-call rates drop from 30%+ to under 5%.
Is the after-hours call figure included automatically?
Yes. The calculator includes a separate after-hours multiplier based on your practice area. You can also enter a custom after-hours call percentage if you have data from your phone system. The output breaks out business-hours vs after-hours revenue leakage separately.
What does an AI receptionist cost compared to the revenue it recovers?
Most Ontario law firms recover the cost of an AI receptionist within the first 30–60 days from a single additional retained client. LexScale.ai AI receptionists start at a fixed monthly fee with no per-call charges, making the math straightforward — book a strategy call for current pricing.

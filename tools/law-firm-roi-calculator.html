What This Tool Does

The Law Firm ROI Calculator helps Ontario law firms quantify the financial return of investing in AI marketing. You input your current monthly lead volume, average client value, client acquisition cost, and practice area — and the calculator models projected ROI across three scenarios: AI SEO alone, AI SEO plus chatbot, and a full-stack deployment including AI receptionist and GBP optimisation. The output includes a 12-month projection, payback timeline, and cost-per-client comparison against your current spend.

Why It Matters for Your Law Firm

Most law firms invest in marketing on instinct rather than numbers. The result is wasted budget — paying $400+ per lead on Google Ads while organic and AI search channels sit untapped. This calculator gives you a defensible business case for AI marketing investment based on your firm's actual numbers, not industry averages. It also lets you model the compounding effect of AI marketing: unlike paid ads that stop working when you stop paying, AI SEO and content compound over time, meaning the ROI curve gets steeper as months pass.

What You'll Get

After completing the calculator, you'll receive a PDF summary showing: projected leads per month by month 3, 6, and 12; estimated cost-per-client under your current model vs AI marketing; total investment required; payback period; and a channel-by-channel breakdown. The report also flags which practice areas in Ontario have the highest AI search opportunity based on current query volume data.

Frequently Asked Questions

Questions About This Tool

What inputs does the calculator need?
The calculator needs four inputs: your current monthly inbound leads, your average client value (retainer or settlement), your current cost per lead (Google Ads, referrals, or total marketing spend divided by leads), and your practice area. Everything else is calculated automatically using LexScale.ai benchmark data from Ontario law firms.
How accurate are the ROI projections?
Projections are based on median outcomes from LexScale.ai client data across Ontario law firms. Individual results vary based on practice area competitiveness, website starting point, and content quality. The calculator uses conservative estimates — actual results have exceeded projections in 68% of client engagements.
What is a realistic payback timeline for AI marketing?
For most Ontario law firms, AI marketing investment pays back within 4–8 months. AI chatbots and receptionists typically pay back in 60–90 days from reduced lead leakage. AI SEO content has a longer runway (3–6 months to rank) but delivers compounding returns for years.
Does the calculator account for different practice areas?
Yes. The calculator uses practice-area-specific data for conversion rates, average client values, and competition levels. Personal injury, family law, criminal defence, and corporate law all have different ROI profiles — the calculator adjusts projections accordingly.
Can I use the results to justify budget internally?
Absolutely. The PDF report is designed to be shared with managing partners or finance teams. It presents projections conservatively, cites the methodology, and includes a sensitivity analysis showing best-case, base-case, and conservative outcomes.

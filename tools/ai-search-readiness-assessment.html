What This Tool Does

The AI Search Readiness Assessment is a 25-question diagnostic that evaluates your law firm's current position across five dimensions of AI search optimisation: website technical health (5 questions), content depth and topical authority (5 questions), schema markup and structured data (5 questions), Google Business Profile optimisation (5 questions), and AI citation signals — reviews, mentions, and authority links (5 questions). Each dimension is scored out of 20, giving a total readiness score out of 100. The assessment takes approximately 8 minutes to complete.

Why It Matters for Your Law Firm

AI search engines — ChatGPT, Google Gemini, and Perplexity — use fundamentally different ranking signals than traditional Google search. A firm can rank on page 1 of Google while being completely invisible in AI responses, or vice versa. The readiness assessment identifies exactly which dimensions are holding your firm back from AI search visibility and prioritises them by impact. Without this diagnostic, most firms either over-invest in areas that don't move the needle or miss the highest-leverage opportunities entirely.

What You'll Get

You receive a personalised PDF report with: your total AI search readiness score (0–100), scores for each of the 5 dimensions, a traffic light status (red/amber/green) for each of the 25 individual criteria, a prioritised 90-day action plan specific to your practice area and current score, benchmark comparison against Ontario law firms in your practice area, and an estimated AI search traffic opportunity if you reach a score of 80+.

Frequently Asked Questions

Questions About This Tool

What score do most Ontario law firms get?
Based on assessments completed by LexScale.ai clients before onboarding, the average Ontario law firm scores 34 out of 100 on the AI Search Readiness Assessment. Personal injury and family law firms tend to score slightly higher due to more competitive markets pushing earlier adoption. Corporate law firms average the lowest scores despite often having the highest-value clients to gain.
How long does the assessment take?
The 25-question assessment takes 7–10 minutes to complete. Questions are structured as yes/no or multiple-choice, not open-ended — no writing required. You'll need access to basic information about your website, Google Business Profile, and current marketing activities.
What dimensions does the assessment cover?
Five dimensions: (1) Website technical health — speed, mobile, HTTPS, schema; (2) Content and topical authority — article depth, FAQ content, practice area pages; (3) Structured data — Organization, Attorney, FAQPage, BreadcrumbList schemas; (4) Google Business Profile — completeness, reviews, posts, Q&A; (5) Citation signals — review velocity, directory listings, authoritative mentions.
What happens after I get my score?
You receive your personalised report immediately after completing the assessment. The report includes a 90-day action plan broken into quick wins (week 1–2), medium-term improvements (month 1–2), and strategic investments (month 2–3). LexScale.ai also offers a free 30-minute strategy call to walk through your results and answer questions.
Is the assessment free?
Yes, completely free. There is no obligation to engage LexScale.ai services. The assessment is designed to give every Ontario law firm a clear, honest picture of where they stand in AI search — whether or not they choose to work with us.

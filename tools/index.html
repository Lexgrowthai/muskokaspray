Frequently Asked Questions

Everything law firms ask about our free AI marketing tools

Are these AI marketing tools really free?+
Yes. All tools in the LexScale.ai toolkit are free to use with no account required for the diagnostic and calculator tools. Some advanced reporting features may require a free account in the future, but the core assessments will remain free.
Which tool should my law firm use first?+
We recommend starting with the AI Search Readiness Assessment to get a baseline score across all dimensions of your AI search presence. From there, the AI Visibility Checker and Google Business Profile Score Checker provide the most actionable quick wins for most Ontario law firms.
How accurate are the ROI and revenue calculators?+
The Law Firm ROI Calculator and Missed Call Revenue Calculator use industry benchmarks derived from real Canadian law firm data. Results are estimates based on your inputs and typical conversion rates for your practice area. They are directionally accurate and useful for business-case modelling.
Do I need technical knowledge to use these tools?+
No. Every tool is designed for law firm owners and managing partners without any technical background. You answer plain-language questions or enter basic business metrics — the tool does the analysis and explains every result in plain English.
How often should I run these assessments?+
We recommend running the AI Visibility Checker and AI Search Readiness Assessment quarterly. The ROI and revenue calculators are most useful when evaluating a new marketing investment or reviewing your annual budget. The GBP Score Checker should be run whenever you make changes to your Google Business Profile.
What happens after I complete a tool assessment?+
Each tool generates a personalised report with your score and a prioritised action plan. For a deeper implementation strategy tailored to your firm's specific practice areas and location, you can book a free strategy call with the LexScale.ai team.
